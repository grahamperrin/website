How to mirror NomadBSD

What to mirror

rsync.nomadbsd.org::nomadbsd/

Expected mirror size

The mirror won't require more than 50GB.

Mirror update frequency

Once a day

How to mirror

rsync -vaHz --delete rsync.nomadbsd.org::nomadbsd/ /target/directory/

Announce a new mirror

If you have created a mirror for NomadBSD let us know via mirror [at] nomadbsd [dot] org
